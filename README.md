## Brainrot Fighters

A fighting game with brainrot mechanics, featuring:
- Multiple characters with unique abilities
- 67 BOSS Survival Mode - Can't damage the boss, survive until it defeats itself
- Shop system with upgrades
- Touch controls for mobile/tablet
- Parry system with cooldown
- Hidden healing mechanics in 67 BOSS mode

### Website: https://iankingsigma.github.io/tekken-8-website/

### Controls:
- **Arrow Keys**: Move left/right
- **Z/X**: Punch attacks
- **A/S**: Kick attacks
- **Space**: Parry (heals 10% HP, deals 20% damage, 10s cooldown)
- **C**: Special combo attack

### 67 BOSS Survival Mode:
- Boss stuns every 5 seconds and loses 10% HP
- Player can't damage the boss - must survive
- Hidden healing: Player heals to 100% every 5 seconds
-
